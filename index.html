Our Mission

To reconize the potential in each child,
To facilitate a child's journy of self-exploration as an individual,a social being and a global citizen.
To encourage learning by doing and provide a balanced curriculum to ensure holistic development of a child- physical,psychological, moral, spiritual and intelectual.
To develop a sprit of inquisitiveness in our children.
To look beyond the horizon and seek a future for our children in the changing global scenario.
